Prepress is a tool for quickly fixing text formatting

Features

  • Remove Vertical Tabulation: Replaces all vertical tab characters with standard line breaks.
  • Remove Non-Breaking Spaces: Finds and replaces hidden non-breaking spaces with regular spaces.
  • Reset Paragraph Spacing: Sets "Space before" and "Space after" paragraph intervals to 0.
  • Extend Formatting: Automatically applies bold style and yellow background to punctuation marks if the preceding word is formatted.
  • Fix Links: Extends hyperlinks to punctuation marks or removes them from trailing commas and semicolons.
  • Extend Tooltip Highlights: Extends turquoise background color to adjacent asterisks (*).

How to Use

  1. Open a document in Google Docs™.
  2. Go to Extensions > Prepress.
  3. Choose one of the options:
    • Click Start Prepress to run all tasks instantly.
    • Click ⚙️ Constructor to open the sidebar and select specific tasks.
