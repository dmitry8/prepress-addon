Prepress — Google Docs Text Formatter

Automatically format your documents to ensure consistent layout and style.

How Prepress Works

Prepress is a Google Docs add-on that streamlines text formatting and ensures consistent document layout. It automatically:

  • Removes vertical tabs and replaces them with line breaks
  • Resets spacing before and after paragraphs for uniform appearance

All formatting happens directly inside your Google Docs document. No data is stored, collected, or shared outside your Google account.

How to Use

  1. Open your Google Docs document
  2. Click the Prepress menu in the toolbar
  3. Select Run Prepress to format your document automatically
