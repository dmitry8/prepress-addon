Prepress — Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Google Docs add-on is developed by an independent developer to help users automatically format text inside their own Google Docs documents.

2. Data Usage

The add-on processes document content locally within the user’s Google account. We do not store, collect, log, or transmit any personal data to any external servers.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share any user data with third parties.

4. Data Control

Users can remove the add-on or revoke its access at any time via their Google Account → Security → Third-party access settings.
